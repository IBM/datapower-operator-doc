## License reference tables

The tables below can be used to determine the appropriate value of the DataPowerService `spec.license.license` property, based on the desired version (`spec.version`) and usage (`spec.license.use`).

### Licenses by channel

|Value of `spec.version`|Value of `spec.license.use`                   |Value of `spec.license.license`|Link to license       |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------|-------------------------------|----------------------|
|`10.0-cd`              |`production`, `nonproduction`, or `developers`|`L-RJON-C5SF54`                |https://ibm.biz/BdfVxG|
|`10.0-cd`              |`developers-limited`                          |`L-RJON-C5SF7N`                |https://ibm.biz/BdfVxn|
|`10.0-lts`             |`production`, `nonproduction`, or `developers`|`L-RJON-C6UNTE`                |https://ibm.biz/BdfeqF|
|`10.0-lts`             |`developers-limited`                          |`L-RJON-C6UNQ4`                |https://ibm.biz/BdfeqE|

<InlineNotification>

When `spec.version` selects a channel, the license corresponds to the latest version within that channel. See [channels](/apis/datapowerservice/v1beta3#channels) for reference.

</InlineNotification>
